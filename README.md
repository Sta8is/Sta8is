I build systems that **predict the future** — specifically, how visual scenes will evolve over time. My PhD focuses on semantic future prediction and latent-space world models, using transformers, self-supervised learning, and vision foundation models. I am a PhD Candidate at NTUA and fellow of [Archimedes Research Unit](https://archimedesai.gr/) / Athena RC.
 
## 🔬 Research Interests
 
- **Semantic future prediction & world models** — forecasting world evolution in learned latent spaces
- **Vision foundation models** — leveraging and extending large-scale pretrained visual representations
- **Generative models × self-supervised learning** — bridging generation and representation learning
- **Machine unlearning** — selective knowledge removal in deep networks
